# Parent Notice

## Purpose
Scholesa provides a learning platform for students, educators, and families. This notice explains what data is used, why it is used, and how parents can exercise their rights.

## Data We Collect
We collect account data, learning activity records, mission artifacts, and limited telemetry needed to support educational outcomes and product safety.

## How AI Is Used
AI features are used to support tutoring, feedback, and classroom workflows. AI guardrails are applied to block unsafe content and cross-tenant data requests.

## Parent Rights
Parents may request access, correction, export, or deletion of a learner's personal data according to applicable school and privacy requirements.

## Contact
For privacy and data requests, contact your school administrator or Scholesa support at privacy@scholesa.com.
