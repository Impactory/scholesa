# Parent Privacy Notice (Template)

Scholesa provides educational services on behalf of schools. This template is intended for schools/districts to share with parents/guardians.

## Information collected (examples)
- Student identifiers provided by the school (e.g., name, class/grade, school email if used)
- Learning progress and work product (missions, checkpoints, reflections, portfolio artifacts)
- System logs needed to secure and operate the service
- AI interaction logs (educational context, governed by safety policies)

## How information is used
- To provide instruction, feedback, and progress tracking
- To support teacher oversight and learning analytics
- To secure, maintain, and improve system reliability

## What we do not do
- No behavioral advertising
- No selling of student information
- No cross-service tracking for marketing

## Data sharing
We may use vetted service providers (e.g., cloud hosting, authentication, AI providers) to operate Scholesa. Data is shared only to provide the educational service and is governed by contractual and technical safeguards.

## Parent/guardian rights
Parents/guardians may request access, correction, or deletion of a child’s information through the school/district. The school will coordinate requests with Scholesa support.
