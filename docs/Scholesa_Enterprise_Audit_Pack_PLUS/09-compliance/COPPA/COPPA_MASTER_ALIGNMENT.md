# COPPA Master Alignment (Operational)

Date: 2026-02-23

Scholesa supports COPPA compliance operationally via the **School Consent (School-as-Agent) model** for educational technology deployments.

This is not legal advice; it is an implementation and evidence framework.

## Coverage summary
1) Notice: Parent Privacy Notice template provided (for school distribution).
2) Consent: School agreement + onboarding checklist supports school-agent consent model.
3) Minimization: Grade-band feature gating and AI prompt minimization.
4) Security: Encryption, RBAC, tenant isolation, monitoring, incident response.
5) Access/Deletion: Parent request workflow through the school + export/delete runbooks.
6) Advertising: No behavioral ads; no sale of student data.
7) Retention: Concrete schedules with district override options.
8) Vendors: AI vendor disclosure template and vendor register.
