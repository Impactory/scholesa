# No Advertising / No Behavioral Profiling Policy

Scholesa does not:
- Display third-party advertisements to students
- Track students across unrelated services for marketing
- Sell student information
- Use student information for behavioral advertising or marketing profiles

Analytics are limited to educational and operational metrics.
