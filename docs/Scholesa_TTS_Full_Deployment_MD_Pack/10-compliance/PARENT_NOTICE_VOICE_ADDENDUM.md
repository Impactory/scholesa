# Parent Notice — Voice Features Addendum (Template)

Scholesa includes voice features to support learning accessibility and engagement.

## What voice features do
- Students can speak to ask questions and explain reasoning
- Scholesa can read instructions and feedback aloud

## What data is processed
- Short-lived audio for transcription and speech playback
- Transcripts used to provide the requested learning assistance

## What we do not do
- No voice cloning of children
- No advertising based on voice
- No sale of voice data
- No external vendor processing for voice features

## Retention
- Audio is short-lived and auto-deleted
- Operational metadata retained for security and reliability
